Exploring Additional Treatment Options

Ketamine Therapy: Given the recent attention ketamine therapy has received for treatment-resistant depression, this may be something you have questions about. Dr. Allen is available to discuss ketamine therapy with you, explain how it works differently than traditional antidepressants, and help you understand whether it might be a beneficial approach for your specific situation. This is one of many treatment considerations that can be explored during your consultation.

Information We Collect

When you provide your phone number for SMS communications with Allen Psychiatry, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Message delivery status and timestamps
  • Message content (appointment reminders, confirmations, practice updates)
  • Your responses to our messages
How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number and SMS data exclusively to:

  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Provide important practice updates and notifications
  • Send treatment-related communications when necessary
  • Improve our communication services
Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your phone number or SMS data to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With your explicit written consent
  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our patients
Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your SMS data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure storage protocols
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and updates
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying "STOP" to any message
  • Request access to your SMS data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and medical record retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive:

  • Appointment reminders (typically 24-48 hours before your appointment)
  • Appointment confirmations
  • Practice updates (infrequent)
  • Treatment-related communications (as needed)
Opt-Out Instructions

To stop receiving SMS messages:

  • Reply "STOP" to any message
  • Call us at (801) 555-0123
  • Email us at info@allenpsychiatry.net
