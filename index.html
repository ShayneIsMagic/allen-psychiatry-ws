SMS Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you provide your phone number for SMS communications, we collect:

  • Your phone number

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number and SMS data to:

  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Provide important practice updates
  • Send treatment-related communications
  • Improve our communication services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your phone number or SMS data to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • As required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your SMS data, including encryption and secure storage protocols.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time
  • Request access to your SMS data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
