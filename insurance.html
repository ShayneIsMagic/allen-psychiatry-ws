Insurance Accepted

We accept most major insurance plans. Verify your coverage and learn about payment options.

Accepted Insurance Plans

Allen Psychiatry is committed to making mental health care accessible. We accept the following insurance plans:

  • Select Health
  • EMI
  • DMBA
  • PEHP
  • Blue Cross/Blue Shield

Verify Your Coverage

Coverage varies by plan and individual policy. We recommend:

  • Contact your insurance provider to verify that Allen Psychiatry is in-network for your specific plan
  • Check your benefits to understand your copay, deductible, and coverage limits
  • Call our office at (385) 504-1334 to discuss payment options and insurance questions

Payment Options

In addition to insurance, we also accept:

  • Cash payments
  • Credit card payments
  • Debit card payments

If you have questions about payment options or need to discuss a payment plan, please contact our office. We're here to help make mental health care accessible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance plan?

We accept Select Health, EMI, DMBA, PEHP, and Blue Cross/Blue Shield. However, coverage can vary based on your specific plan. We recommend contacting your insurance provider directly to verify that Allen Psychiatry is in-network for your plan.

What if my insurance isn't listed?

If your insurance plan isn't listed, please call our office at (385) 504-1334. We may still be able to work with your insurance provider, or we can discuss other payment options.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, a photo ID, a list of current medications, and any pre-visit paperwork that was sent to you.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that mental health care is important and want to make it accessible. Please contact our office to discuss payment plan options that work for you.

Ready to Schedule an Appointment?

If you have questions about insurance coverage or payment options, we're here to help. Contact us today to verify your coverage and schedule your appointment.
