Dr. Todd Allen, DNP, PMHNP-BC

A dedicated mental health professional committed to providing compassionate, evidence-based care to individuals and families across Utah County and rural communities.

Professional Background

Dr. Todd Allen, DNP, is a Board Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC) serving Provo, UT, and the broader Utah County area, including rural communities that often face limited access to specialized mental health care. With his Doctor of Nursing Practice specializing in psychiatry, Dr. Allen brings advanced clinical expertise to individuals ages 14 to 55, with a primary focus on ADHD treatment in adolescents and adults. He also provides comprehensive care for depression, anxiety, PTSD, and the list goes on.

Research & Academic Contributions

His commitment to advancing psychiatric care extends beyond clinical practice through his sleep-study research and his role as a member of the research team with Boeson, contributing to innovative approaches in mental health treatment.

Treatment Philosophy

He emphasizes strong therapeutic partnerships with each client, believing that authentic compassion and active listening form the foundation of effective mental health care, especially for those managing ADHD and related conditions.

Dr. Allen integrates the latest evidence-based practices in ADHD treatment while staying current with emerging developments in psychiatric care, ensuring his patients receive comprehensive, holistic treatment options tailored to their unique needs.

Community Focus & Understanding

Throughout his distinguished career, Dr. Allen has observed the far-reaching effects of mental health conditions on individuals, families, and communities across Utah County, particularly in underserved rural areas where specialized mental health care, including ADHD treatment, is difficult, inconvenient, with long distances needing to be traveled or often unavailable.

Community Advocacy & Personal Connection

Beyond his clinical expertise, he understands the persistent stigma surrounding mental health treatment. He is passionate about educating communities on the vital importance of mental health care and how transformative quality treatment can be for individuals and families. Having grown up in rural communities himself, Dr. Allen holds deep respect for the resilience, hard work, and strong values found in these areas, and he is committed to bringing specialized ADHD care to those who may have previously lacked access to such services.

Patient Care Commitment

Dr. Allen is honored to support your mental health journey, providing a welcoming, non-judgmental space where you can feel heard, understood, and empowered to achieve lasting positive change.
