TeleHealth Services

Convenient and confidential mental health care from anywhere

Professional TeleHealth Services in Utah

At Allen Psychiatry, we understand that getting to an in-person appointment isn't always possible or convenient. Whether you're managing a busy farming schedule, caring for family, or simply prefer the privacy of your own home, our TeleHealth services bring expert psychiatric care directly to you.

Mental Health Care That Fits Your Life

Our TeleHealth platform provides the same high-quality mental health care you would receive in our Provo office, delivered through secure video technology that respects your schedule and privacy. We recognize the unique challenges faced by rural communities and busy professionals, and our virtual services bridge these barriers effectively.

TeleHealth at a Glance

  • Platform: Secure, HIPAA-compliant video conferencing technology
  • Availability: Same appointment times as in-office visits
  • Services: Full range of psychiatric evaluations and treatments
  • Accessibility: Works on any device with camera and microphone
*All TeleHealth services meet or exceed in-office care standards

Our TeleHealth Services Include

We offer comprehensive mental health services through our secure TeleHealth platform, ensuring you receive the same level of care as in-person visits.

  • Initial Psychiatric Evaluations: Comprehensive assessments to understand your mental health needs and develop personalized treatment plans
  • ADHD Assessments: Thorough evaluation and testing for attention deficit hyperactivity disorder
  • Medication Management Appointments: Expert guidance on psychiatric medications, dosage adjustments, and monitoring
  • Follow-up Consultations: Regular check-ins to track progress and adjust treatment as needed
  • Crisis Interventions: Immediate support for urgent mental health situations
  • Therapeutic Support Sessions: Ongoing therapy and counseling services

Getting Started with TeleHealth

Our streamlined process makes it easy to begin your TeleHealth journey with Allen Psychiatry.

  • Schedule Your Appointment: Use our online scheduling system or call our office to book your TeleHealth session
  • Receive Your Secure Link: We'll send you a secure, private link via email with simple instructions
  • Connect and Begin Care: Click your link at the scheduled time on any device with a camera and microphone
  • Continue Your Journey: Follow-ups, medication management, and ongoing support are all available through our platform

Technology Requirements

Our TeleHealth platform works on computers, tablets, and smartphones. You'll need a stable internet connection, a camera, and a microphone. We'll provide detailed setup instructions before your first appointment.

Who Benefits from TeleHealth?

TeleHealth services are ideal for various individuals and situations, providing convenient access to quality mental health care.

  • Rural Community Members: Farmers, ranchers, and families who face long travel distances to access mental health care
  • Busy Professionals & Parents: Those with demanding schedules who need flexible appointment options
  • Individuals Seeking Privacy: People who prefer the discretion of receiving care in the comfort of their own home
  • Those Needing Consistent Care: Patients requiring regular follow-ups without the barrier of travel time and costs
  • Individuals with Mobility Issues: Those who have difficulty traveling to in-person appointments
  • Weather-Dependent Scheduling: Patients in areas where weather can make travel difficult or dangerous

Privacy and Security

Your privacy is our priority. Our TeleHealth platform uses enterprise-grade encryption and is fully HIPAA-compliant. All sessions are confidential and secure, meeting the same standards as in-office visits.

Ready to Take Your First Step?

If you're ready to begin your journey toward accessible mental health care, Dr. Allen is here to help. Schedule a consultation to learn more about how telehealth services can support you.
