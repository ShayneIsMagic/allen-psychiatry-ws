## ‚úÖ **ARE CHANGES WARRANTED? YES**

### **Why These Changes Matter:**

#### **1. Title Length (60-Character Rule)**
- **Google displays ~60 characters** in search results
- Titles over 60 chars get truncated with "..."
- **Impact:** Users see incomplete titles, lower click-through rates

**Examples:**
- Current: "Depression Treatment Provo | Treatment-Resistant Depression | Ketamine Therapy | Accepting New Patients" (95 chars)
- **What Google shows:** "Depression Treatment Provo | Treatment-Resistant Depression | Ket..." ‚ùå
- **What you want:** "Depression Treatment Provo Utah | Treatment-Resistant Depression" (60 chars) ‚úÖ

#### **2. "UT" vs "Utah"**
- **"Utah" is more searchable** - people search "Provo Utah" not "Provo UT"
- **Better for local SEO** - full state name is clearer
- **Impact:** Slightly better match for search queries

#### **3. "Dr. Todd Allen" in Title**
- **Brand consistency:** Your brand is "Allen Psychiatry"
- **Space efficiency:** Removes 15 characters
- **Impact:** More room for keywords, cleaner branding

#### **4. Rural Page Title**
- **Current:** "Rural Services - Allen Psychiatry" (34 chars, no keywords)
- **Problem:** Doesn't tell search engines what it's about
- **Impact:** Poor SEO, low search visibility
